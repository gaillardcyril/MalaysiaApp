Malaysian Students’ Council of Australia, Victoria (MASCA Victoria)

Overview

The Malaysian Students’ Council of Australia, Victoria (MASCA Victoria) is the peak representative body for all 9000 Malaysian students in Victoria, Australia, also playing a national leadership role for more than 20,000 Malaysian students in Australia via its value adding events and initiatives.

MASCA Victoria visions to provide a platform in nurturing future leaders and in promoting the spirit of Malaysia amongst the Malaysian community and the wider Australian community and missions in representing and serving the interest and welfare of all Malaysian students in Australia.

MASCA Victoria is proud to have close partnership eight affiliated student bodies in Victoria and the support from Education Malaysia and the Consulate General Malaysia Melbourne.

Apart from that, it also spearheads the ASEAN-Australia Agenda, which aims to create a strong community of Southeast-Asian youth leaders in Australia and to strengthen the ASEAN-Australia relations, especially at the youth leadership level.
